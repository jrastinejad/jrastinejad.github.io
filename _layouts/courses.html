International Gemini Observatory/NOIRLab/NSF/AURA/M. Zamani; NASA/ESA

In 2021, I led a large, international team that discovered a new kilonova following a long-duration gamma-ray burst. The above image shows our discovery and follow-up with Hubble (P.I. Rastinejad) and Gemini. Our analysis found that a mass of heavy elements equal to 5 percent of the Sun was produced.
