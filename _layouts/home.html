I am a Presidential Fellow and Astronomy Ph.D. Candidate at Northwestern University.

Research

I leverage rapid-response observations from premiere telescopes (like Gemini-North Observatory, pictured here!) to study the astrophysical origins of the heavy elements, including silver and gold. My research aims to answer questions such as: What are the explosions that create heavy elements? What can these explosions teach us about black holes and neutron stars? When did the heavy elements - including those necessary for life on Earth - enter our Universe?
